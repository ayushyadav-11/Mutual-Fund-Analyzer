🧭 How to Read These Metrics
📊 Volatility
How wildly the NAV swings. Rated vs your fund's peer category — so a 25% volatility is Low for Small Cap but High for Large Cap.
⚡ Sharpe Ratio
Return you earned per unit of risk. A rating of Good means your reward fairly compensates the risk taken vs the risk-free rate (6.5% RBI).
🛡️ Sortino Ratio
Like Sharpe, but only punishes downside moves. Better suited for volatile equity funds — a high Sortino means you lose money rarely.
📉 Max Drawdown
The worst peak-to-trough loss in 3 years. Mild = survived market crashes well. Severe = significant losses during downturns.
📡 Beta
Sensitivity to the correct benchmark (not always Nifty 50 — Banking funds use Nifty Bank, etc.). Defensive <1 means less market sensitivity.
🏅 Peer Rank
How your fund compares to other funds in your own portfolio of the same type. #1 = best risk-adjusted return (Sharpe) among similar funds you hold.
